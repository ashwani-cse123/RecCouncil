Rajkiya Engineering College, Gonda is a prestigious government engineering institution located in the heart of Uttar Pradesh. Established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh, the college aims to provide quality technical education and foster innovation, research, and development.

The college offers undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. With a focus on academic excellence and holistic development, REC Gonda is committed to shaping the next generation of engineers and technocrats.

The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, smart classrooms, and a library enriched with technical resources. Regular workshops, technical fests, and industrial training opportunities keep students industry-ready and innovative.
