Welcome to Technical Council REC Gonda

The Technical Council is a student body responsible for promoting technical culture on campus. It organizes coding competitions, hackathons, workshops, and manages tech clubs like robotics, AI/ML, and development. It helps students explore and grow in tech, innovate, and prepare for careers through hands-on learning and events.
